Our Methodology

How We Deliver Excellence

From raw material selection to final installation support, our process is engineered for perfection. We don't just ship tiles; we deliver certainty.

01

Precision Manufacturing

Our Morbi facility combines Italian engineering with Indian craftsmanship. 1200C kilns, 4500-ton presses, and digital inkjet printers with 400 DPI resolution ensure every tile meets global standards.

01.1

Raw Curation

Silica, clay, and feldspar from certified Gujarat & Rajasthan mines. XRF analysis ensures chemical purity before sub-micron grinding.

<45m Particle 6-7% Moisture
01.2

Spray Drying

10-meter dryers atomize slip into granules. Sacmi Continua+ presses apply 4500 tons750 elephants per square meter.

4500T Force 2.4g/cm Density
01.3

Digital Decoration

System 8-channel inkjet printers at 400 DPI. Italian pigments bond molecularly for decades of fade resistance.

400 DPI 8-Channel
01.4

Firing & Vitrification

120-meter kilns at 1200C 2C. Quartz dissolves, feldspar forms glass, rapid cooling locks density.

1200C <0.05% Absorption
02

Quality Control

Triple-inspection protocol ensures zero defect tolerance. We don't inspect quality; we engineer it into every molecule.

02.1

Raw Material Testing

XRF spectrometry analyzes chemical composition. Moisture content, particle size distribution, and plasticity index verified before production.

02.2

In-Process Monitoring

Real-time density checks, dimensional laser scanning, and visual defect detection at every production stage.

02.3

Pre-Shipment Verification

Mohs hardness, abrasion resistance, thermal shock, and chemical exposure testing. ISO 9001:2015 certified protocols.

03

Secure Packaging

Custom-engineered protection systems prevent breakage across continents. Your tiles arrive as pristine as they left our kilns.

03.1

Internal Protection

PE foam inserts cradle each tile. Corrugated separators prevent edge contact. Moisture-barrier shrink wrap eliminates humidity damage.

03.2

Crate Engineering

ISPM-15 certified wooden crates with reinforced corners. Shock-absorbent base padding. Steel banding for structural integrity.

03.3

Container Optimization

Custom loading plans maximize space utilization. Dunnage bags prevent shifting. Desiccant controls humidity during ocean transit.

04

Logistics Mastery

Just-in-time delivery to 50+ countries. Real-time tracking, customs clearance support, and white-glove handling from Morbi to your site.

04.1

Route Optimization

AI-powered shipping algorithms select optimal ports and carriers. Multi-modal options: sea, air, and land freight coordination.

04.2

Documentation Excellence

Complete export documentation: COO, CI, PL, BL, and certificates. Customs pre-clearance reduces port delays by 70%.

04.3

Last-Mile Coordination

Delivery scheduling aligns with construction timelines. Unloading supervision ensures damage-free receipt. Installation support available.
